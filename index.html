About Me

I am a dynamic and versatile professional with expertise spanning geography, climate change research, GIS/Remote Sensing, sound engineering, and web development. My academic and project background in geography and GIS equips me to analyze spatial and environmental data to address pressing challenges such as sea level rise and sustainable land management.

In addition to my technical foundation, I design and build user-friendly digital solutions, including an e-commerce website, a Tesla configurator page, and a sushi restaurant platform, showcasing my ability to blend creativity with technical precision. My adaptability and dedication to excellence allow me to contribute meaningfully to projects that make life easier, more sustainable, and more efficient for clients and stakeholders.

I am passionate about applying my multidisciplinary expertise to tackle global challenges like climate change, while also leveraging technology to deliver real-world impact.
